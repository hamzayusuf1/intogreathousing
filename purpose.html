About Us

Our Mission & Vision

At Intogreat Housing, we are dedicated to empowering migrants and refugees to forge brighter futures and become integral members of their communities. It's crucial to recognise the challenges faced by those seeking asylum in the UK, especially considering the disheartening statistic that people who have been granted asylum in the UK have a disproportionately low employment rate of 51 percent.

Despite these obstacles, our mission remains steadfast. We envision a society where everyone, regardless of their background, has equitable access to safe housing, meaningful employment, and opportunities for personal growth and success. Our comprehensive support services are designed to address the multifaceted needs of individuals transitioning to life in the UK.

Our Principles

Learn about the guiding principles for our work

Empowerment through Opportunity

Through access to education, training, and employment support, we empower individuals to regain control of their futures.

Resilience and Compassion

We approach our work with resilience and compassion, recognizing the strength and resilience of asylum seekers in the face of adversity.

Integration and Community Connection

We are committed to fostering integration and connection within communities, bridging gaps and breaking down barriers between asylum seekers and their new environments.

Promotion of Equality and Equity

We are dedicated to promoting equality and equity for asylum seekers, advocating for fair treatment and access to opportunities regardless of background or status.
